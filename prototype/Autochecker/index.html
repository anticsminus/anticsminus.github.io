Welcome to Autochecker Garage!

Located in the heart of Cornwall, Ontario, Autochecker Garage has been your go-to destination for all things automotive. Since our inception, we've prided ourselves on providing top-notch service, exceptional workmanship, and a dedication to ensuring every vehicle that drives into our garage receives the best care possible.

  • Expertise: Our team is equipped to handle a wide range of automotive issues, big or small.
  • Transparency: We provide clear, upfront estimates and keep you informed every step of the way.
  • Quality: We use only high-quality parts and materials to ensure lasting repairs and maintenance.

Our Mission: At Autochecker Garage, our mission is simple: to deliver quality, honesty, and transparency in every service we offer. Whether it's a routine oil change, a complex engine repair, or a thorough diagnostic, we treat every vehicle as if it were our own.

Our Team: Our team of experienced and certified technicians brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to every job. With continuous training and the latest diagnostic tools at our disposal, we stay ahead of the curve to ensure your vehicle is always in optimal condition.

Community Commitment: We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us throughout the years. Autochecker Garage is proud to participate in local events, sponsor youth sports teams, and contribute to charitable causes within Cornwall.

About

Just another human being, fascinated by clouds and animals who enjoys passing the time programming and learning new things. I was born in Langley British Columbia, was raised in Edmonton Alberta, and currently reside in Cornwall Ontario Canada where I recently finished studying game programming. Studying game programming has allowed me to really develop my passion for programming in itself as a whole. Whether working on UI/UX, gameplay/engines, or even creating 3D models I find myself consistently losing track of time due to being immersed in the projects I am currently working on. I enjoy working with team-members and empowering them to strive and complete tasks assigned to ensure we can make our deadlines for the sprints ahead. I guess you can say I’m just another technical guy who enjoys video games and seeing things reanimate through programming/code and game engines.
